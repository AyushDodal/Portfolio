Customer Churn Prediction

A full statistical modeling project predicting telecom customer churn. It includes EDA, model building (Logistic Regression, Random Forest, XGBoost), model interpretation, and an interactive dashboard for stakeholders.

Key Results

  • Baseline Logistic Regression AUC: ~0.82
  • XGBoost AUC: ~0.90 (after hyperparameter tuning)
  • Top predictors: Contract type, tenure, payment method, monthly charges

Business Impact

This model helps identify high-risk customers for targeted retention campaigns, improving customer lifetime value and reducing churn-driven revenue loss.
